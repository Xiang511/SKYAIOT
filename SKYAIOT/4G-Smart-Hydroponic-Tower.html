4G Smart Hydroponic Tower

4G Smart Hydroponic Tower

Transform any small space into a smart farm with this innovative, fully automated growing system. Its modular design is not only easy to clean and maintain but also ensures a hygienic, soil-free growing environment that is pest-free. Thanks to 4G connectivity and a 4-input controller, you can manage settings remotely from your phone using the Smart App Control, no Wi-Fi needed. The precision watering system saves water while ensuring your plants thrive.

Enjoy fast and clean harvests of fresh, chemical-free greens, making it perfect for home use and effortless daily gardening. Beyond the home, its applications are vast. Businesses can use it for store displays to blend nature and technology, while restaurants can offer ultra-fresh, sustainable greens to their customers. It's also an ideal system for education, providing a hands-on way to teach sustainability and farming. With dimensions of 63 cm (W) x 197 cm (H), a 65 L water tank, and a voltage of 110-220 V, this device makes growing easy for everyone, anywhere.
