Privacy Policy

若您對本隱私權政策有任何疑問，歡迎與我們聯繫。

This application (hereinafter referred to as "this application") respects and protects your privacy. When you use the camera function of this application,
we will follow the following privacy policy to ensure the security of your personal information.
This application needs to access your device's camera so that you can use the "SKYAIOT APP" function. This application will only access your camera when you actively use the relevant function, which may allow you to capture images or videos, but these contents are only stored locally on your device and will not be automatically uploaded to any server. This application will not use your images or videos for any other purpose, nor will it share them with any third party, unless with your explicit consent, required by law, or to protect our or others' rights, property, or safety.
You can revoke the camera permission of this application at any time in the device settings. If you refuse to authorize the camera permission, some functions may not work properly.
This application will not collect, store, or transmit your camera images or videos to any remote server. All images and videos are only retained on your local device.
If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please feel free to contact us.
