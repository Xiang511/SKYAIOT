4-channel sensor controller

The 4-channel industrial controller is available in three versions: WiFi, Ethernet, and 4G IoT card. It features 4 relay switches capable of withstanding voltages up to 1500V. The controller includes 5 sets of digital inputs for sensor data and supports seamless interactivity among sensor values. With a 1-second response time, the controller ensures real-time feedback with low-latency data. The 5 sets of switch outputs allow for the control of physical switches, facilitating easy field linkage. Its wide range of applications makes it suitable for both domestic and international use. Adopting an integrated control approach, this controller provides a versatile solution for various scenarios.
