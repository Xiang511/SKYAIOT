Coffee Tree Cultivation

Tianliao District, Kaohsiung City

Analyzing sensor data collected in the cloud, we study the concentrated flowering patterns of coffee trees to effectively enhance coffee bean yields. The benefits of time and labor savings are achieved through remote operation via a mobile app. Furthermore, leveraging this research data allows for a reanalysis of how to control the flavor and characteristics of coffee.

Smart Flower Garden

A-Lian District, Kaohsiung City

This area collaborates with the Sunrise Radiance Center to establish a solar power station for electricity supply and a simplified smart agriculture system with water-saving drip irrigation. This system, compatible with courses offered by the Radiance Center, allows the elderly to easily cultivate crops with just a few taps, enhancing their sense of accomplishment after harvest
